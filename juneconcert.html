June 2, 2003 


Dear Orchestra Members,

Thanks for all your hard work. The concert will be June 11, and it promises to be very exciting.

LOCATION: The auditorium of Eckstein Middle School, 3003 NE 75th. From the community center, go north to 75th, turn right, and go up the hill to 30th. The school is on your right (south side). There is parking in front, on the west side, and in a lot on the east side of the school. Enter the main door, and you can’t miss the auditorium.

DRESS: Anything white on top, anything black on bottom

SCHEDULE: We will have a warm up/rehearsal prior to the performance. The Monday orchestra should arrive at 6:45 to help set up, tune and begin our last short rehearsal at 7:00 sharp; the Wednesday orchestra should be ready to rehearse by 7:20. Note that this is different from last time. Plan to arrive a little early so that we can tune with extra care. Please keep your music with you when you’re not playing, so that it doesn’t get mixed up with the other orchestra. We’ll open the door to your friends and relatives at 7:55 and begin the concert at 8 pm. After the performance we will have refreshments. The Monday orchestra should contribute something simple to drink for the refreshment table. The Wednesday orchestra should bring something to eat. Note that this is reversed from last time.


Many little tasks need to be done before and after the rehearsal/concert, and we would be appreciative if some of you would volunteer 3-5 minutes of your time to help. The Monday players could help by setting up chairs on the stage, passing out programs or getting the lighting figured out, The Wednesday orchestra players could help by setting up the table for refreshments and and restoring the stage to its original configuration after the concert. If everyone would be responsible for taking home any extra food or drink brought, the general cleanup will be very simple. Thanks in advance for your help.

We will take the summer off. In late August the fall schedule will be set and you will get an e-mail announcement of the beginning dates. It will be in the later half of September. If you know you will or won’t be joining us please tell us; it helps with planning music. Also, if you change your e-mail address please let us know.

Have a wonderful summer!


Judy and Lorraine
