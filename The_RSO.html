The Ravenna String Orchestra
   

This advanced group is composed of about thirty members. There are no auditions, and all ages are welcome. The rehearsals are informal and without pressure, however, members of the orchestra are serious about preparing for the short concerts which conclude each quarter. Most of the music we play is from the standard repertoire.  The Updates page will give you an idea of current pieces. If you need more information about which orchestra you should join please ask us through the Contact page. Joseph Pollard White is the conductor of the advanced orchestra. Seattle Parks and Recreation sponsors the orchestra.

Rehearsals are on Wednesday evenings, 7:15 - 8:30, at the Ravenna-Eckstein Community Center, 6535 Ravenna Ave. NE, Seattle. The cost is $75 per session. Please bring a stand.  Go to Updates for exact information about the current or next session.
