Updates


FALL SESSION:

The next concert will be at 8 p.m. on Wednesday, December 10, at the Eckstein Middle School auditorium, 3003 NE 75th Street.

The Ravenna Second String Orchestra will begin Monday rehearsals on September 15 and continue through December 8. The Ravenna String Orchestra (Wednesdays) will begin on September 17 and run through December 10 which will be the concert date for both orchestras.  Please e-mail Judy Drake to request PDF files of the music.

Repertoire for the Monday orchestra will be an arrangement of Grave and Allegro by Telemann, Two Folk Dances by Chen and Roubanis arranged by Lauren Bernofsky, and Drifen by Shirl Jae Atwell. The Wednesday orchestra will be preparing the first movement of Leclair’s Violin Concerto in A Minor, “Allegro Moderato” (first movement) from Mozart’s Symphony #29, Nocturne (from String Quartet No. 2) by Borodin and Dance of the Trolls by Mike Forbes.

For pdf files of the music please e-mail Judy Drake for parts.

The fee per quarter to participate is $75.  Please contact us if you would like to apply for a scholarship.

Sign up through the Ravenna-Eckstein Community Center:
  • in person (6535 Ravenna Ave. NE),
  • by phone (206-684-7534), or
  • by going to the website: https://class.seattle.gov/parks/Activities/ActivitiesAdvSearch.asp  (Scroll down to Performing Arts & Dance and click on Music - Group Lessons)
