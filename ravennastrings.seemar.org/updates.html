SPRING SESSION 

The Ravenna Second String Orchestra (Mondays) will begin on April 2 and run through June 4 (with no rehearsal on Memorial Day, May 28). The Ravenna String Orchestra (Wednesdays) will begin on April 4 and run through May 30. The concert date for both orchestras will be on Wednesday, June 6, at Eckstein Middle School.

For pdf files of the music please e-mail Joe White for parts.

The fee per quarter to participate is $75.  Please contact us if you would like to apply for a scholarship.

Sign up through the Ravenna-Eckstein Community Center:
  • in person (6535 Ravenna Ave. NE),
  • by phone (206-684-7534), or
  • by going to the website: https://class.seattle.gov/parks/Activities/ActivitiesAdvSearch.asp  (Scroll down to Performing Arts & Dance and click on Music - Group Lessons)
