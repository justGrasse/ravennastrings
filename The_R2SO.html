The Ravenna Second String Orchestra

   
This orchestra of about thirty members is on the intermediate level and provides a rewarding way to improve your ensemble playing.  Itâ€™s a little easier than the Ravenna String Orchestra.  There are no auditions, and all ages are invited to join.  We have fun while preparing for the short concerts at the end of the quarter.  The music is chosen from arrangements of average difficulty or compositions written for intermediate players.  To see a recent program go to the Updates page.  Nathan Harrison conducts the Intermediate Orchestra. Seattle Parks and Recreation sponsors the orchestra.

Rehearsals are on Monday evenings, 7:15 - 8:30, at the Ravenna-Eckstein Community Center, 6535 Ravenna Ave. NE, Seattle.  The cost is $75 per session. Please bring a stand.  Go to Updates for exact information about the current or next session.
